My goals

Being an animation major has made me consider goals that will allow me to use my gifts and help others. One goal I have is to create stories that resonate with me and others. Specifically, I want these stories to focus on my experiences with growing up and mental health, and I hope to attract audiences with similar experiences to inspire conversations and awareness on specfic topics. My second goal is to create an animation studio for fellow creatives. With the current practices in the industry, I want to create a safe space for other artists to share their stories while also valuing their time and efforts. Because of the rise of independent animation, I believe that artists can be heard and respected if they have the platform to do so, and I want to contribute to that.

My final goal can overlap with animation since the medium thrives on experiences. Since I can only gain these experiences through living, my final goal is to live life to the fullest. Throughout high school, I rarely made time for my interests and friends and mainly focused on school work. One thing college taught me is that I can balance both. Because of my extracurriculars and club socials, I was able to meet new people and try new things; these experiences inspire new ideas for my animations everyday. Therefore, I hope to keep maintaining this balance, not just for my career, by for my wellbeing as well.
